# The Beaver's Choice Paper Company Needs Your Help!

## 1. Introduction

Welcome! Imagine yourself as a trusted and seasoned consultant specialized in building smart, efficient, and powerful agent-based workflows. Businesses rely on you to solve their complex operational challenges with cutting-edge solutions. 

Your latest client, **The Beaver's Choice Paper Company**, urgently requires your expertise to revolutionize their inventory management and quoting system. Your role? Develop a multi-agent system that streamlines their operations, enabling quick quote generation, accurate inventory tracking, and ultimately driving increased sales.

## 2. The Challenge

The Beaver's Choice Paper Company is struggling with:
- Managing their paper supplies
- Responding promptly to customer inquiries
- Generating competitive quotes

They're overwhelmed and losing potential sales due to inefficiencies. 

**Your challenge:** Design and implement a multi-agent solution, **restricted to at most five agents**, capable of:
- Handling inquiries
- Checking inventory status
- Providing accurate quotations
- Completing transactions seamlessly

Your solution must ensure **responsiveness, accuracy, and reliability** in managing requests and maintaining optimal stock levels.

## 3. Core Components of the Multi-agent System

### 3.1 System Requirements

To maintain simplicity and effectiveness, the system should involve **at most five agents**. Your solution will:
- Handle strictly **text-based inputs and outputs**
- Be verified using a provided set of sample requests
- Begin with a **workflow diagram** that clearly outlines your intended workflow

### 3.2 Framework Choice

You may choose among three Python frameworks for your agent implementation:
- **smolagents**
- **pydantic-ai**
- **npcpy**

### 3.3 Agent Capabilities

Your completed program should contain agents that can:

1. **Inventory Management**
   - Answer questions regarding current inventory
   - Manage the reordering of supplies when necessary
   - Use database information effectively
   - Make purchase decisions

2. **Quote Generation**
   - Provide accurate and intelligent quotes for potential customers
   - Consider historical quote data and pricing strategies

3. **Sales Transactions**
   - Efficiently finalize sales transactions
   - Consider available inventory and delivery timelines

## 4. Submission Requirements

Your submission must include:

1. **The workflow diagram** (an image file)
2. **Your source code** (only one python file should be submitted)
3. **A detailed document** that thoroughly explains:
   - The system you've built
   - How you ensured it meets all specified requirements

## 5. Technical Stack

You'll utilize:
- **Multi-agent systems** knowledge
- **Python programming** skills
- **Database management** (sqlite3)
- **Agent orchestration framework** of your choice

## 6. Project Summary

This exciting **6-hour project** is divided into key steps:

### Step 1: Diagramming & Planning
Create a detailed flow diagram outlining your multi-agent system.

### Step 2: Implementation
Develop your multi-agent system using Python and your chosen agent framework.

### Step 3: Testing & Debugging
Thoroughly test your agents using provided sample inputs to ensure robust performance.

### Step 4: Documentation
Write a comprehensive report explaining your system, your design decisions, and how your solution meets the project requirements.
